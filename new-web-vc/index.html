About

From Concepts to Creations

Hi, and welcome to my website!
I'm a Bsc.CSIT student with a passion for technology, creativity, and education. I'm currently diving deep into subjects like Data Structures & Algorithms, AI, Machine Learning, networking, Computer Architecture, Statistics, and Computer Graphics—both theoretically and through practical labs using tools like PSPP, MATLAB, C/C++, Python, SQL, and VHDL. Along with a group of five friends, I’m working on a project called Pahelo Kagaj—a platform designed to support Nepali students through accessible online resources and, eventually, virtual classes. Our goal is to build something meaningful that empowers learners across Nepal. I also enjoy exploring tools like ADB to manage Android systems more efficiently, especially for streamlining devices like my Android devices. On top of that, I’m working on an advanced weather prediction app that uses multiple free APIs, integrates geolocation, and provides 5-day forecasts with intuitive UI features. Learning from textbooks is important to me—I aim to build a solid foundation in every subject I study. At the same time, I love building real-world applications that combine theory and practice. When I’m not coding, debugging, or working on educational tools, you might find me brainstorming new ideas or helping friends solve tricky problems. Thanks for visiting! If you're interested in tech, education, or collaboration, feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect.
